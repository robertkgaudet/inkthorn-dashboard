🌐 §5 — Public Business Website Direction
Not generic. Not template. Not Bootstrap. Per-business design that makes users feel like they hired an agency. Three templates auto-selected by industry. Lighthouse 90+ on mobile. Static HTML, no JS framework.
ModernSoft serif headlines, generous whitespace, single accent color. Default: service businesses, consultants, professional services.
BoldHeavy display type, high-contrast color blocks. Default: product brands, lifestyle, retail.
EditorialMagazine-style layout, image-forward. Default: creative, food, hospitality, content businesses.
Required: Calendly embed (optional), Schema.org + llms.txt + sitemap.xml auto-generated, OG + Twitter card, mobile-first responsive. Launch: slug.app.textos.ai — custom domain V1.5.
