I'm a Silicon Valley-caliber engineer. But building TextOS, I didn't use any of that. I was more of a poet.

FAQ

Real questions. Straight answers.

01 How does TextOS actually work? ›

You type one sentence describing your business. TextOS runs an 8-phase agent build: it researches your market, names your brand strategy, builds a live website at yourhandle.textos.ai, writes your 30-day content calendar, drafts platform bios, creates a pitch outreach email, and queues your first tasks — all while you watch in a live terminal. The whole build takes under five minutes. You get real artifacts, not drafts.

02 Who is TextOS for? ›

Solopreneurs, freelancers, consultants, coaches, photographers, small business owners — anyone who has the idea and the skill but keeps hitting the assembly bottleneck. Especially good if you've been saying "I need to build my online presence" for six months and haven't started yet. Also strong for agents or brokers who need to spin up personal brands fast, and small teams who want to manage multiple clients without managing six separate tools.

03 Who is TextOS NOT for? ›

People who want to spam generic AI content with no real perspective behind it. Enterprise teams that need custom CRM integrations and approval workflows. People who are already running a content operation and just need a scheduler. If you already have a team, a site, and a working system — TextOS probably won't add much. It's built for people who are starting from zero or starting over.

04 Does the free plan actually build a live site? ›

Yes — genuinely. No teaser, no wall at step three. The full 8-phase build runs on the free plan and your site goes live at yourhandle.textos.ai with no expiration. The difference with paid plans isn't the build — it's execution. Free plan gives you everything built. Paid plans let the agent execute tasks, post content, send outreach, and run ongoing work on your behalf.

05 What's the Telegram integration? ›

Connect your Telegram account and your entire TextOS dashboard becomes a conversation. You can approve tasks, trigger content posts, check your site, run new builds — all from a text message on your phone. No app to open, no browser required. It's designed for people who live in their phone and don't want to log into a dashboard every time they need to do something.

06 Is the Founder Lifetime rate really locked forever at $9.99/mo? ›

Yes — as long as your subscription stays active. Pay $49.99 once to lock the rate, then $9.99/month from month four onward, permanently. When all 1,000 founder slots are gone, the public starting rate is $29.99/month. The rate does not increase as long as you don't cancel. If you cancel and re-subscribe later, you re-enter at the public rate at that time.

07 Do I need to install anything or know how to code? ›

No. TextOS is a web app. Sign in with Google or a magic link — no password to set, nothing to configure. The agent runs in your browser. Telegram is optional and takes about 60 seconds to connect. If you can write a sentence, you can use TextOS.
