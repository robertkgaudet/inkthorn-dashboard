Your door-to-door flow

Three beats.
Every conversation.

You don't need a pitch deck. You don't need a presentation. Just walk in, be genuine, and run the tool. The numbers do the convincing.

🚶
Beat 1 — Walk in
Introduce yourself

Keep it simple. "Hi, I'm Shaun — I work with InkThorn, a local AI company. We do free AI readiness checks for businesses in the area. Mind if I show you something quick?" That's it. No pressure, no pitch.

📱
Beat 2 — Pull up the tool
Run the scan together

Open this page on your phone, tap the diagnosis link, and type in their business with them watching. It takes 60 seconds. When their score comes up — let them read it. Silence is your friend here.

📅
Beat 3 — Book the call
Hand them the next step

After the score, ask: "Want to see exactly what it would look like to fix this for your business?" Then send them the Calendly link or book it right there on your phone. Your job is done.
