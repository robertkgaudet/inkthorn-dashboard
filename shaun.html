Your door-to-door flow

Three beats.
Every conversation.

Beat 01
Walk In

Introduce yourself simply. Don't pitch — just open the door with a question they can't say no to.

"Hey, I work with a company called InkThorn — we help local businesses show up when people search on AI like ChatGPT and Google. Mind if I show you something real quick on my phone?"
Beat 02
Pull Up the Tool

Open the diagnosis on your phone. Enter their business name right in front of them. Let the AI generate their scores live. Let the silence do the work.

"Watch this — I'm going to type in your business name and let the AI tell us exactly where you stand. Takes about 60 seconds."
Beat 03
Book the Call

Once they see their score, don't pitch — offer a conversation. Rob handles the close. Your job is just to get the calendar open.

"The guy who built this tool — Rob — does a free 20-minute call. He'll walk you through exactly what this means for your business. Want me to get you on his calendar?"
