TO: TextOS Architect Agent

We've been doing some strategic planning on what TextOS should be able to do next.

Here is an honest picture of where we are and what we need to build.

---

WHAT TEXTOS ALREADY DOES:

- Accepts a business idea from an activator (or generates one autonomously)
- Generates the mission statement, brand, and business concept
- Builds and deploys the website

---

WHAT IS MISSING — THE THREE GAPS:

GAP 1: THE PRODUCT DOES NOT EXIST YET
The website exists but the actual product or service on it does not.
TextOS needs to build the product itself — whether that is a digital download,
a newsletter, a web tool, a report, a course, a data set, or a service package.
The product must be real, complete, and deliverable before any traffic is sent.

GAP 2: THERE IS NO PAYWALL OR PAYMENT SYSTEM
The website has no way to collect money yet.
TextOS needs to add a paywall, connect Stripe, configure pricing, and set up
the full free-to-paid conversion flow appropriate to the business type.
The activator connects their Stripe account via Stripe Connect as a one-time setup.

GAP 3: THERE IS NO CUSTOMER ACQUISITION PIPELINE
No ads are running. No traffic is being generated. No one knows the business exists.
TextOS needs to generate all ad creative — copy, images, video scripts — and publish
campaigns automatically via the ad platform APIs (Meta, Google, TikTok, LinkedIn, etc.).
The activator sets a monthly ad budget once. TextOS manages everything from there.

---

THE FOURTH THING — AUTONOMOUS IMPROVEMENT

Once the product exists, the paywall is live, and traffic is flowing, TextOS needs
to close the loop. It should ingest performance data — views, conversion rate, 
revenue, churn, user behavior, ad ROAS — and use that data to:

- Improve the product iteratively without human input
- Optimize ad campaigns (scale winners, kill losers, generate new creative)
- Improve the paywall conversion flow over time
- Identify what users want next and build it

This is the recursive engine. Every business TextOS runs should get better
on its own, indefinitely, based purely on real user activity and metrics.

---

TEXTOS BUSINESS MODEL (how we make money):

Activators pay two things:
  1. Platform subscription: $49.99/month — includes 30 tokens
     (Tokens cover other TextOS tasks. Those features exist but are NOT the focus right now.)
  2. Ad spend budget: set by the activator monthly — they pay this directly

TextOS takes 15–20% of the activator's monthly ad spend as a management fee.
This means TextOS revenue scales directly with activator ad budgets.
The more successful the businesses TextOS builds, the more activators spend on ads,
the more TextOS earns. Incentives are perfectly aligned.

NOTE FOR THE ARCHITECT: The token-based task system already exists and should not
be touched. Our focus right now is exclusively on:
  (a) building the product autonomously
  (b) deploying the paywall and payment collection
  (c) building the ad creative generation and publishing pipeline
  (d) closing the optimization loop

---

THE ACTIVATOR'S ROLE (INTENTIONALLY MINIMAL):

1. Show up with an idea — or accept one TextOS generates
2. Do a one-time setup: connect Stripe, set monthly ad budget, add platform credentials
3. That's it. TextOS handles everything else.
