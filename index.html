People Are Stopping Going to Websites. They're Getting Answers From AI.

Your customers aren't browsing websites anymore. They're asking AI. "Best pizza in New Orleans." "Good hair salon in Lafayette." "Auto shop near me in Baton Rouge." ChatGPT answers. Google's AI answers. Claude answers. The human never visits a website. The AI does — and it decides who to recommend.

This means your website is no longer a destination. It's a source document that AI reads. And AI is looking for something completely different from what web designers have spent 20 years building. It doesn't care how your site looks. It cares how your content is structured — whether it can extract who you are, what you do, where you are, and why you're the best answer to the question someone just asked.

A beautiful $5,000 Squarespace site with no GEO structure will lose to a plain, well-written page that speaks AI's language. We build the latter. On purpose.
