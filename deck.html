Live Demo 1 of 4 · Gmail + Calendar

The Morning Briefing

The command
"Check my Gmail for anything urgent, pull my calendar for today, and write me a briefing doc."
What Claude does: Opens Chrome → scans Gmail unread → reads your calendar → produces a formatted briefing with priority items and your day laid out.

You do: Nothing. You come back to the result.

Live Demo 2 of 4 · Gmail Drafts

Write It. File It. You Approve It.

The command
"Write a follow-up email to today's Beta in Progress attendees — thank them, mention TrustLight.ai, and save it to my Gmail drafts so I can review before it goes out."
What Claude does: Drafts the email in your voice → opens Chrome → navigates Gmail → composes and saves to Drafts. Nothing sends without you.

Why it matters: Claude does the writing and the filing. You stay in control of what actually leaves your inbox.
