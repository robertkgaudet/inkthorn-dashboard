Why This Exists

Ian Changed Everything for the Gulf Coast

On September 28, 2022, Hurricane Ian made landfall near Fort Myers as a Category 4 storm with 150 mph winds. It was the deadliest Florida hurricane since 1935.

What followed the storm was its own disaster. Overwhelmed insurance carriers. Delayed adjusters. Disputed claims. Homeowners living in trailers on their own properties for a year or more. Some never came back at all.

The families who recovered fastest weren't the ones who got lucky — they were the ones who had documentation. Who could show an adjuster exactly what they had before the storm took it.

KAVO360 was built so that the next family doesn't have to learn that lesson the hard way.
