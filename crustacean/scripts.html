📞 DAILY CALL SCRIPTS — YOUR WORK STARTS HERE

Pick a script. Open the leads dashboard. Start dialing. This is the page you come back to every day.

The GEO Pitch in one sentence:
"If you're not showing up when people ask ChatGPT for a [business type] in [city] — you're invisible to a massive number of customers. We fix that, and we already built your site for free."
SITUATION: Business has NO website
OPENING — LEAD WITH GEO
"Hi, is this the owner of [Business Name]?

Hey, my name is [Your Name] — I'm with InkThorn, we're based here in Louisiana.

Quick question — when someone opens ChatGPT or Google's AI and asks for a [salon / restaurant / auto shop] in [city] right now — does your business show up?

[Pause. Let them answer.]

That's exactly what I thought. AI search is sending customers to businesses every day — and without a GEO-optimized website, you're not in that conversation at all. Your competitors who have one are getting those customers instead of you.

Here's the thing — we already went ahead and built you a free demo site that's GEO-ready. You can see it right now at inkthorn.ai — just type your business name in the search bar at the top.

Can you pull that up real quick while I have you?"
Key: Lead with AI invisibility — not the free site. The demo is proof of the solution, not the pitch itself.
IF THEY SAY "I'M LOOKING AT IT NOW"
"That's your business — we built that from your Google listing. It's got your address, phone, services, and it's already structured so AI search engines can read it and recommend you.

To keep it, update it, and make it fully yours — it's $95 a month. No contract, cancel anytime.

Right now, if someone asks ChatGPT for a [business type] in [city] — you don't show up. With this, you do.

Want to go ahead and claim it?"
IF THEY ASK "WHAT DOES IT COST?"
"We have three plans. Most businesses go with the Growth plan — $95 a month. There's also a Starter at $47, and a Premium with online ordering at $179.

But pull it up first — see what we built for you — and then we can figure out which plan fits. The site is already done. You just have to decide if you want it."
SITUATION: Business already has a website
OPENING — GEO WEDGE
"Hi, is this the owner of [Business Name]?

My name is [Your Name] with InkThorn. I know you already have a website — I actually looked at it. It looks good.

My question is: does it show up when someone asks ChatGPT or Google's AI search for [business type] in [city]?

[Pause. Let them answer.]

Most websites built before 2024 don't — not because they're bad, but because GEO optimization barely existed yet. It's a completely new layer of search that most web developers don't even know about.

We specialize in that. We call it GEO — Generative Engine Optimization. It's the difference between showing up when someone Googles you versus showing up when someone asks AI for what you do.

Can I take 60 seconds to show you what the gap looks like?"
Key move: Offer to do a live ChatGPT search while on the phone. Ask them to open ChatGPT and type "[business type] in [city]." If they don't show up — that moment does all the selling for you.
IF THEY SAY "MY SITE IS FINE / JUST REDONE"
"That's great, and it's probably solid for traditional Google search. What I'm asking about is specifically AI search — ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity. Those launched in the last 2 years and they work completely differently.

Even a brand-new website won't be GEO-ready unless whoever built it specifically added that structure. Most designers don't even know it exists yet.

Want me to run a quick free check and tell you if your current site is AI-ready or not? Takes 5 minutes and costs you nothing."
SITUATION: No answer — leaving a voicemail
VOICEMAIL — UNDER 25 SECONDS
"Hey, this is [Your Name] with InkThorn — calling for the owner of [Business Name].

Quick question: does your business show up when someone asks ChatGPT for a [business type] in [city]? If not, we built you a free demo site that fixes that.

Check it out at inkthorn.ai — search your business name. Call me back at [Your Number]. Thanks."
Why it works: "Does your business show up on ChatGPT" is a question they've never been asked. Curiosity will make them search. Keep it under 25 seconds — every extra second reduces callback rates.
SITUATION: Sending a text message
SMS — FIRST TEXT
"Hi, I'm [Name] with InkThorn. Does [Business Name] show up when someone asks ChatGPT for [business type] in [city]? We built you a free GEO-ready site to fix that — search your name at inkthorn.ai. $95/mo, no contract. 🦞"
SMS — FOLLOW-UP (Day 3 if no response)
"Just following up — the free demo site we built for [Business Name] is live at inkthorn.ai. AI search is sending customers to businesses daily — make sure you're in the conversation before your competitors are."
SMS Rules: Send 10am–4pm only. One follow-up maximum. If no response after 2 texts, move on. Quality over volume — never mass-blast unsolicited.
SITUATION: Called before — following up
FOLLOW-UP CALL — DAY 2 OR 3
"Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] again from InkThorn — I left you a message a couple days ago about the AI search issue for [Business Name].

Did you get a chance to look up your business on inkthorn.ai?

[If yes:] Great — what did you think? Any questions about claiming it?

[If no:] No problem — pull it up real quick right now, just takes a second. Type inkthorn.ai and search your business name. I'll wait."
FOLLOW-UP — THEY SAW IT BUT DIDN'T COMMIT
"Hey [Name], just checking back — you mentioned you saw the site last week. I wanted to see if anything was holding you back.

Most owners I talk to wonder if it's worth $95 a month. Here's how I think about it: if this site brings in even one new customer a month, it's paid for itself multiple times over.

Are you ready to go ahead, or is there a specific question I can answer?"
SITUATION: They're interested — time to close
THE CLOSE
"To get started, I just need your email address and we'll get you set up on the Growth plan — $95 a month, first month to try it out, cancel anytime.

I'll send you a link right now to complete the setup — takes about 2 minutes. What's the best email to use?"
Always end with a specific next step. Never leave it open. "What's your email?" moves forward. "Let me know if you're interested" dies.
UPSELL — IF THEY CHOOSE STARTER ($47)
"The Starter plan is solid — you'll be on the map for AI search from day one. One thing to consider: the Growth plan at $95 adds a services page and booking integration, which is what really drives inbound calls.

Most restaurants and service businesses go Growth. But Starter is a great starting point — we can upgrade any time."
