How referral tracking works:
Each team member picks their own unique word (up to 6 characters) — like JORDAN, MIKE, or SALES1. Their referral link becomes https://inkthorn.ai/?ref=JORDAN.
When a prospect visits using that link, the code is stored in their browser. Every sign-up form and "Claim Site" button on the site will automatically include ?ref=MARCUS01 in the URL — so you can track which member drove the conversion.

To close a sale manually: Have the team member direct the prospect to inkthorn.ai/?ref=THEIRCODE — or share the link from the table below.
