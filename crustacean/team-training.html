The Process

5 Steps From Lead to Close

Follow these steps every time. Don't skip steps. The demo site is your secret weapon — use it early.

1

Find Your Lead

Open the Partner Dashboard. Pick a business with a phone number. Look for ones with good ratings (4+ stars) — they care about their reputation and are more likely to want a professional online presence. Filter by city or category to work an area systematically.

2

Pull Up Their Demo Site First

Before you call, click "View Demo Site" from the dashboard. Look at it. Know the business name, category, and address. This makes you sound informed and personal — not like you're reading from a list. The URL is inkthorn.ai/crustacean/demos/[business-name]/

3

Make the Call (Use the Script Below)

Keep it short. Your only goal on the first call is to get them to look at their demo site. Don't try to close on the first call — just get them curious. The site does the selling.

4

Follow Up

If they looked at the site, follow up within 24 hours. If they didn't answer, call back twice more on different days and different times. Most closes happen on the 2nd or 3rd contact. Don't give up after one attempt.

5

Close & Submit

When they say yes, direct them to inkthorn.ai to sign up, or collect their info and submit on their behalf. Log your close so you get credit for your commission.

Pro Tips

What Separates Good Closers From Great Ones

🎯
Call during business hours, not at opening or closing. 10am–12pm and 2pm–4pm are the sweet spots. Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons.
📊
Lead with ratings. If a business has 4.7 stars and 200 reviews, mention it: "You have a 4.7 rating with 200 reviews — that's impressive. We want to make sure new customers can find you online."
🗣️
Talk less. Ask more. Your job is to ask questions and listen. The more they talk, the more they sell themselves. "How do most of your new customers find you right now?" is gold.
🔢
Work in batches of 20. Call 20 leads in a row before stopping. Your energy and delivery improve with repetition, and your close rate on calls 15–20 will be higher than on calls 1–5.
📝
Log everything. Note whether they answered, heard the voicemail, saw the site, or said no. Follow-ups close more deals than first calls. Most "no's" become "yes" on contact 2 or 3.
💬
Never argue. If they're not interested, thank them and move on. There are 6,500 leads. Spend your energy on the ones who are curious, not the ones who aren't.
🦞
Believe in the product. This is real. The demo sites exist. The technology works. When you call knowing you have something genuinely valuable to offer, it comes through in your voice — and people say yes.
