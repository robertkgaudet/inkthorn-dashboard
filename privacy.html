Privacy Policy

InkThorn Crustacean Web Development · A division of InkThorn.ai
Effective Date: March 24, 2026 · Last Updated: March 24, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We keep it short and plain — no legal jargon walls.

1. Who We Are

InkThorn Crustacean is a web development service operated by InkThorn.ai, based in New Orleans, Louisiana. We build and maintain websites for small businesses. Contact us at admin@inkthorn.ai.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Give Us

  • At checkout: Name, business name, email address, phone number, and payment information (processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card number)
  • By email: Any content, photos, or instructions you send to admin@inkthorn.ai for website updates
  • By SMS: Your phone number and any replies you send to our outreach messages

2.2 Publicly Available Business Data

To build website previews, we use publicly available information about your business including your business name, address, phone number, hours, photos, category, and reviews from Google Maps and similar sources. This data is already public — we organize it into a professional website format.

2.3 Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our website, we may collect standard web analytics data including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time on site. We use this to understand how people use our site and improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To build, launch, and maintain your website
  • To process your subscription payments via Stripe
  • To communicate with you about your website (updates, confirmations, support)
  • To send you relevant service communications and, with your consent, marketing messages
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. Ever.

4. SMS Communications

If you received an SMS from us, your phone number was sourced from a public business directory (such as Google Maps). Our SMS messages are business-to-business outreach about our web development services. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. We will honor all opt-out requests immediately and will not contact you again by SMS.

We do not share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Payment Information

All payment processing is handled by Stripe. We never see, store, or have access to your full credit card number. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

6. How We Share Your Information

We only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Stripe — to process payments
  • Service providers — hosting, email delivery, and analytics tools that help us operate the service, all under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of InkThorn.ai or others

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your subscription is active and for a reasonable period afterward for legal and business purposes. If you cancel and request deletion of your data, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS or email marketing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email admin@inkthorn.ai.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption on all pages and secure third-party payment processing via Stripe. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable precautions to protect your data.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify active subscribers of material changes by email. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

12. Contact

Privacy questions or requests? Email admin@inkthorn.ai.
