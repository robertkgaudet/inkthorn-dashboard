How It Works

Three steps to a clear path forward

No account. No email. No upsell. Just honest coaching built around your specific situation.

01

Choose Your Path

Running for office or advocating for an issue. Two completely separate question tracks — both designed by people who've been in the arena.

02

Answer 7 Questions

Quick multiple-choice questions about your situation — timeline, resources, experience, challenges. Plus one open-ended question about why you're doing this.

03

Get Your Roadmap

A personalized 3-phase roadmap, a deep-dive on your #1 challenge, targeted warnings based on your situation, and essential resources to move forward.

What You Get

A real guide, not generic advice

Everything adapts to your answers. A first-time city council candidate gets a different roadmap than a statewide incumbent. An underfunded grassroots organizer gets a different strategy than a well-resourced coalition.

🗺️

3-Phase Roadmap

A concrete phase-by-phase action plan calibrated to your timeline and resources. Not a generic checklist — built around where you are today.

🎯

Priority Focus

A deep-dive on your biggest challenge — fundraising, message, team, visibility, or opposition. Practical tactics, not platitudes.

⚠️

Targeted Warnings

Honest flags based on your situation — underfunded campaigns, solo advocates, first-timers, industry opposition. Know what you're up against.

📚

Essential Resources

The specific tools, trainings, and filings you actually need — not a generic list of everything that exists in politics.
